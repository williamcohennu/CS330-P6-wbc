About Me

My name is Will Cohen and I am a senior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science in the McCormick School of Engineering.

During my time at Northwestern, I have gotten work experience in both software enginnering and finance.

The summer after my freshman year I interned at Utility, a mobile app design and development agency. At Utility I helped develop and test mobile applications for a wide variety of early-stage funded businesses, corporations, and government clients. I spent most of my time on two projects: Counslr, a healthcare services startup focused on behavioral health for college students, and AsthMe, a healthcare app created for patients with Asthma.

I spent my sophomore summer at Sagewind Capital, a middle-market private equity firm based in New York City that is primarily focused on the government services industry.

This past summer I worked at Blackstone, and I will be returning to the Liquid Credit Group after graduation.
