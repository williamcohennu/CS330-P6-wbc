Everest Reinsurance Co

June 2021

Everest RE ($RE) is primarily in the property and casualty reinsurance business, and currently has a market capitalization of approximately $13bn.

At a high level, reinsurance is insurance for insurance companies, where the goal is to protect against oversized losses. The reinsurer (Everest) agrees to compensate another insurance company against all or a portion of the insurance written by the ceding company.

We see two core drivers of future growth for the company: momentum for premium growth and space for underwriting accuracy to drive value.
